A calming sensory object
for children with emotional trauma
For my final project for Major Studio 1 at Parsons the New School I was looking to use some of the new skills that I have learned in my classes. Using the Arduino, I was able to prototype sound and create an object that would be useful for many children.

The Final Proof of Concept of the Calming Object

Design Questions:
How can I make an enjoyable experience for children so that they feel safe or secure?
How can I create an experience for children that can potentially take away some of their anxieties?
How can the use of senses make a child feel calm?

Users:
Children Ages 7-12

Goal:
To create a human centered design (HCD) object for children with PTSD or anxiety, and by using interaction with the senses the object will take away some of their frustrations. The senses that will be used are sound, smell, and touch. When the child hugs the pillow they will hear sound, smell a calming smell, and feel soft fabric.

Criteria:
1. for children 7-12
2. use senses
3. play sound
4. feel soft
5. smell needs to be relaxing
6. shape and feel needs to be calming
