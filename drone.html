Breezemont Day Camp

Project | Photography and Web Design
Location | Breezemont Day Camp, Armonk, New York
Role | Experience Design, Drone Photographer
Tools | Wood Shop
Team Members | Danielle Sobel, Estee Bruno
Project for | MFADT Fall 2017 Drones and the Third Dimension
Year and Place | 2017 Parsons School of Design

For my drone class at Parsons The New School, I visited and photographed Breezemont Day Camp in Armonk, New York. Breezemont served as a hotel starting in 1911. The hotel (which had 50 rooms) catered to families from New York City who came up to the country. Today, many children from Westchester County, New York attend the camp during summer time.
